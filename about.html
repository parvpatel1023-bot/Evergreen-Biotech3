About Evergreen Biotech

Pioneering sustainable biotechnology since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Bangalore, India, Evergreen Biotech emerged with a vision to harness biotechnology for creating sustainable solutions to global challenges.

With over 200 dedicated scientists and researchers, we have established ourselves as a leading biotechnology company focused on healthcare, agriculture, industrial applications, and environmental conservation.

Our state-of-the-art research facilities serve as the epicenter of groundbreaking work in genetic engineering, synthetic biology, and sustainable biotechnology.
